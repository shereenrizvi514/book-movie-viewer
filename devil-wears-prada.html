Author: Lauren Weisberger ✍

Genres: Fiction Chick lit Humor

Characters: Andrea Sachs, Miranda Priestly, Emily Charlton, Nigel

Summary: Andrea Sachs lands a job as assistant to a powerful editor-in-chief in New York’s high-stakes fashion world. What seems like a dream job soon becomes a toxic challenge to her values, identity, and mental health.
